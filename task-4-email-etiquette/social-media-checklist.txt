Social Media Do’s and Don’t 
  

      DO’S :     

• Think before posting - ensure your content is appropriate and respectful. 
• Protect your privacy – use strong passwords and privacy settings. 
• Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news. 
• Be respectful and polite in comments and messages. 
• Use social media for learning and networking. 
• Keep your profile professional (especially for career opportunities). 
• Report inappropriate content or harmful behaviour. 

 
 
      
      DON’T: 
 

• Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, 
passwords). 

• Don’t post offensive, abusive, or harmful content. 
• Don’t spread rumours or unverified information. 
• Don’t spend too much time on social media during study hours. 
• Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online fights. 
• Don’t click on suspicious links or scams. 
• Don’t post anything that can harm your reputation in the future.
