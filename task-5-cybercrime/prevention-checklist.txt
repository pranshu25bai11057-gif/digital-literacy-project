Prevention Checklist 
 

       Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist: 

• Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (mix letters, numbers, 
symbols) and enable two-factor authentication (2FA). 

• Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone—even if they claim to be from 
a bank or college authority. 

• Be careful with unknown links and attachments in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS 
(they may be phishing scams). 

• Limit personal information on social media (avoid sharing phone number, 
address, college ID, travel plans). 

• Check privacy settings regularly on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and 
LinkedIn. 

• Download apps only from official stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store) and 
review permissions before installing. 

• Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into 
important accounts. 

• Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software. 
 
 

       Financial/UPI Safety Tips: 

• Never scan unknown QR codes or accept random payment requests on apps like 
Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm. 

• Verify UPI IDs before sending money and double-check the receiver’s name. 
• Do not click on fake “refund” or “cashback” links—these are common scams. 
• Immediately block your bank account or UPI if you suspect fraud. 

 
 

       If you face cybercrime: 

• Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 



• Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for immediate financial fraud help)
